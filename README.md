# my-slack-proxy

An always-on Slack proxy — a disclosed, distinct stand-in that covers your Slack while you're away and guards your attention.

It watches the Slack activity relevant to you, filters out the noise, and takes a competent first action in your voice — answering the obvious, surfacing or covering the uncaught-but-relevant, and asking you only when judgment is genuinely required. It is openly not you and never impersonates you, yet carries your context and tone.
